Death Road

Previous Next



This breathtaking, bike ride down the road to Coroico offers the chance to experience the adrenaline rush of hurtling down what has become famously known as the "world's most dangerous road". The adventure begins in the thin air of La Cumbre, at a frigid altitude of 4670m, encircled by the rugged snow capped mountains of the Cordillera Real. We roll down the first part on smoothly paved road while enjoying the peaks of the Andes around us. Halfway down the road narrows and changes to dirt-road - experience the freedom of the vast open spaces as the trail winds its way through a spectacular and bottomless valley into the lush vegetation of the temperate Yungas jungle. On the way you will pass under waterfalls and cross narrow streams. The mountain biking bliss is concluded in Yolosa. From there we drive to a hotel where hot showers, a pool, and a delicious buffet lunch are waiting.

Maximize your comfort and security on Canadian hand-made Rocky Mountain RMX donwhill specific bikes, complete with full suspension,and hydraulic disc brakes and place your trust in our experienced English-speaking guides. Our experience, the quality of service we provide, and the well-maintained bikes guarantee that you can fully enjoy this spectacular downhill ride.

  • Length: Day Tour (11 hours)
  • Ride Time: 5 hours
  • Terrain: 85% Downhill
  • Distance: 64 km
  • Difficulty: All levels
  • Food: Breakfast, Snacks, Water and Buffet Lunch
  • Not included: 50 Bs. Park entrance fee
