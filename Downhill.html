Downhill Madness - mountain biking professionals since 1995

Madness is the only South American company that can offer you mountain biking activities and experiences on Canadian-made Rocky Mountain bikes (we are exclusive Dealer for Bolivia and Peru)

What makes us different?
  • We ride the best bikes in Bolivia, unquestionably! Drop into our office and check them out for yourself, our friendly staff will be more than happy to point out the (huge) differences in quality. Rocky mountain bikes are handmade in B.C. Canada, not in a south-east Asian sweatshop (unlike Kona, Iron Horse, Haro, Trek, etc.). More about our bikes see here.
  • We have more experience! Downhill Madness is Bolivia's pioneer mountain bike guide company and has been two-wheeling around the Bolivian countryside since 1995.
  • Safety. Our logo may be a skull rocking a full face helmet but we have one of the best safety records of all the companies in La Paz, and unlike our competitors, we have nothing to hide! All of our guides are professionally trained in First Aid and C.P.R. as well as rope rescue, plus we have the tools and supplies in our support vehicle to make these skills applicable in the rare case of an emergency.
  • We are 100% Bolivian owned!! Feel content that your money is going to a company that gives back to the local community.
